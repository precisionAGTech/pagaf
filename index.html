example.py
from pagaf import Client

# Initialize client
client = Client()

# Define your field
field = client.create_field(
    geojson="my_field.geojson"
)

# Run NDVI analysis
result = field.analyze(
    crop="corn",
    type="ndvi"
)

# Download prescription map
result.to_shapefile("rx_map.shp")
result.to_geojson()
result.to_geotiff()
Our Mission

Why we do what we do

Facing global climate change, economic uncertainties, a growing population, and increasing urbanization, technology is essential to secure our food supplies.

Precision agriculture is a proven approach, but barriers remain: cost, complexity, vendor lock-in. The mission of PAGAF is to make precision agriculture capabilities available to any farmer who wishes to use it, at no cost.
